About Me

Hey, I’m Natasha. I’m a software engineer, visual artist, day-dreamer based in Boston. I am motivated and inspired by community, healing work, and my bori roots. My education and career background prior to software engineering was centered on uplifting and supporting survivors of sexual and gender-based violence, and working with first-generation and low-income youth. I graduated from Colgate University with a degree in Education and Gender Studies. I have experience working in higher education, mental health services, and youth organizations. My personal life and work experiences have made me deliberate and thorough because there’s often too much at stake to not be. I believe that tech and art are interconnected, especially in reimagining a more just and equitable world. I want to bridge the two worlds and use the skills I’ve learned through software engineering to create magia with others.
